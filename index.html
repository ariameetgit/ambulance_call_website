Research Context

The Reality

Ambulance call centres (like YAS) handle over 1.1 million calls annually. Category 1 calls‚Äîsuch as cardiac arrest‚Äîdemand immediate response.

Experienced handlers often recognise severity within 15-20 seconds. However, accuracy can be impacted by:

  • High stress & call volumes
  • Variability in expertise

This bottleneck potentially delays life-saving interventions.

The Innovation

Emerging AI-driven speech analysis presents a transformative opportunity. By analyzing hidden audio cues:

  • Laboured breathing patterns
  • Vocal markers of severe distress

Integrating these tools enables earlier prediction of Category 1 emergencies, improving decision speed and ultimately saving lives.
